Dr. Stone

The man who saved people from Rockification

Here are some of the timeline of Dr Stone life:

  • Pre-Petrification Era: Senku Ishigami, a brilliant high school student, is passionate about science and technology. The mysterious petrification event occurs, turning all of humanity into stone statues.
  • Post-Petrification Era:Senku awakens after 3,700 years of petrification. He revives his best friend Taiju Oki using the Revival Fluid.
  • Kingdom of Science Arc: Senku establishes the Kingdom of Science with the goal of advancing technology. He revives Tsukasa Shishio, a powerful fighter, but their ideologies clash.
  • Stone Wars Arc: Senku faces off against Tsukasa in a battle for control over the revived humans. The Kingdom of Science allies with Chrome, Gen Asagiri, and others.
  • Village Arc: Senku and his team discover a village led by Kohaku and her sister Ruri. Ruri is sick, and Senku aims to create antibiotics to save her. The village becomes a new base for scientific progress.
  • Treasure Island Arc: Senku sets sail to find the source of petrification on Treasure Island. They encounter Xeno, a scientist from America, and his crew. The battle for knowledge and technology ensues.
  • New America City Arc:Senku and Xeno collaborate to create a communications device. They establish a new city with advanced technology.
